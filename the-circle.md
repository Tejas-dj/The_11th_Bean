## How to add someone

Copy the template block below, paste it after the last `---`, and fill in the fields.
Every field is required except `photo` (leave it blank until you have the actual photo).

```
## Full Name
id: lowercase-with-dashes-no-spaces
category: Music
handle: @instagramhandle
url: https://instagram.com/instagramhandle
tagline: One line — what they do.
since: 2023
color: #2A2320
photo: filename.jpg
shishir_says: Write what Shishir would actually say about this person. No quotes needed here.
```

**Category options:** Music · Photography · Design · Food · Tech · Art · Other

**Color options (pick one for the photo placeholder):**
- Dark espresso (default): `#2A2320`
- Caramel: `#8B6D4A`
- Rattan: `#C8A96E`
- Brick: `#A0674B`
- Sage: `#B8B394`

**Photo:** Shoot an 800 × 1000 px portrait. Drop the file in `/public/circle/` and set `photo:` to the filename (e.g. `ravi-sharma.jpg`). If there's no photo yet, leave the `photo:` line blank or delete it — a solid color placeholder will show instead.
